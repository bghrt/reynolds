About/Mission

J. Sargeant Reynolds Community College’s library will provide an environment where students and faculty can meet their learning and teaching needs and develop lifelong habits of self-improvement, free inquiry and free-expression.

Check it out here!

Group Study Rooms

The Downtown Campus has three group study rooms. Parham Road Campus has eight group study rooms and Goochalnd Campus has one group study room. Each room can accommodate four to six people. The following regulations are designed to provide fair access to the study rooms and to ensure responsible use.

If We Don't Have It

If we don't have it, you could still get it with two options: interlibrary loan or a RALC "Pass".

Library Services

The bottom line is that we're here to support your academic success. Whether you need an book that we don't have or an in-depth research consultation or just a quick online chat session at one in the morning, we "got that".

Chat 24/7

Chat support is covered by Virginia Community College Librarians during the day. After 5:00 pm and on weekends, chat support is contracted out. For campus and Reynolds specific questions, please email us at library@reynolds.edu

Research Consultations

Research consulations are available to both students and faculty members working on serious assignments. If you require more than 30 minutes of research support, making an appointment with a Reynolds librarian is your best bet. We will confirm your request within two business days.
