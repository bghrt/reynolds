Video Request Form (Faculty Only)

Learn more about streaming video with our video guide. Use this form to request streaming videos or DVDs for your classes. If you are requesting a streaming film via SWANK please check their catalog, to ensure they offer it by searching their database. Please note, this video request form should be used by faculty only and only if the video is needed for instructional purposes.

Please allow two weeks for your film request. If Reynolds library does not own the film, it will take over two weeks to purchase the film.
