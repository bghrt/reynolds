Ask A Librarian

Other than stopping by in person, you can contact us in various ways.

Phone the Reference Desk

  • Downtown campus: 804-523-5333
  • Parham campus: 804-523-5329
  • Goochland campus: 804-523-5419

Email

Submit your request for information to a Parham or Downtown campus librarian who will respond to requests usually within 24 hours.

LRC Live

Chat directly with a librarian online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Note: during most regular business hours, this is staffed by VCCS librarians and occasionally J. Sargeant Reynolds librarians. During non-business hours, librarians from around the country provide service.
