About RALC

The purpose of the RALC shall be to further the development of the academic libraries of RALC and to sponsor projects for the mutual benefit of its members. RALC promotes resource sharing through such activities as interlibrary lending, document delivery, direct borrowing by patrons, shared cooperative collection development, and automated networking. See About Us for further history of RALC.
