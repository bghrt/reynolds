Library Assessment

We highly recommend that you require your students to take a library assessment after the library instruction session. The Library Assessment is available in the two following formats:

Print Version:

  • Assessment (PDF) You can print out the assessment and distribute it to your students.
  • Answer Key (PDF) for the print version.

Blackboard version:

  • Zip file Save the .zip file on your local drive and work through the following steps to import and deploy the test into your Blackboard. Tests will be automatically graded upon completion. If you need assistance, please call 523-5329. Thank you.

Step One

  • Save the.zip file for Blackboard on your local drive.

Step Two

  • Enter your course where you would like the Library Assessment to be deployed.
  • Click Control Panel
  • Click Import Package
  • Click Browse and locate the zipped Library Assessment you have saved in Step One.
  • Select the zipped Library Assessment and click Open.

Step Three

  • Checking the following:
    • Settings
    • Tests, Surveys, & Pools
    • Gradebook settings
  • Click Submit when complete

Your students are now ready to take the Library Assessment deployed in Assignments.
