Oceanic Abstracts

Focused exclusively on worldwide technical literature pertaining to the marine and brackish-water environment. The journal is recognized as a leading source of information on topics relating to the oceans, fisheries, aqua culture, meteorology and geology, plus environmental, technological, and legislative topics. (No full text)

Opposing Viewpoints in Context

Draws on the acclaimed social issues series published by Greenhaven Press, as well as core reference content from other Gale & Macmillan Reference USA sources. Access viewpoint articles, topic overviews, statistics, primary documents, links to web sites, and full-text magazine and newspaper articles. (Full text)

OverDrive Digital Media Collection

A digital media collection containing audiobooks, videos, and music. Collection includes over 1000 audiobooks. Media can be downloaded on your home computer, a compatible portable device, or burned to a CD. Audiobook fiction titles include: drama, historical fiction, literature, mystery suspense, poetry, and science fiction & fantasy. Audiobook nonfiction titles include: biographies, current events & politics, and history. (Full media)

Oxford African American Studies Center

Provides access to the three-volume Encyclopedia of African American History 1619-1895, the three-volume Black Women in America, and the highly acclaimed Africana, a five-volume history of the African and African American experience. (Full text)

Oxford English Dictionary

Historical dictionary of English, covering the language from the earliest times to the present day. It shows not only the current meanings of words, but also traces their development through time. Entries contain detailed word origin analysis and are illustrated by quotations from a wide range of English language sources from around the world. (Full-text)

Oxford Medicine Online

Oxford Medicine Online is a collection of online medical resources which cover every stage in a medical career. This database allows users to browse and search a range of online medical textbooks and handbooks from a single location. (Full text)

Oxford Reference

Contains dictionary, language reference, and subject reference works published by Oxford University Press. Includes both 125 core academic subject, language, and quotation dictionaries and 98 in-depth and specialist titles from Oxfordâ€™s award-winning encyclopedias. (Full text)

Oxford University Press

Offers online access to 134 periodical titles in the fields of the humanities, social sciences, and the sciences. The full text of these online journals is generally available for the past several years, though abstracts of articles date back additional years. Articles are available in PDF and/or HTML formats. (Full text)
