RALC Borrowing Pass

The RALC pass provides limited reciprocal borrowing privileges for students, faculty and staff in good standing of member libraries. Borrowers must abide by all policies of the lending library, including loan periods, fines, and replacement charges. Students must obtain a pass at their home library in order to borrow from another RALC institution. Faculty need only show their IDs.
